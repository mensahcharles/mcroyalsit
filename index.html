How an engagement runs

Four stages, every time

Whether it's a penetration test or an ISO 27001 readiness review, the engagement follows the same disciplined sequence.

01 / SCOPE

Define the boundary

We agree exactly what's in scope, what's out, and what success looks like — in writing, before any work begins.

02 / ASSESS

Test or review

Hands-on testing, audit, or implementation work against the relevant standard or framework.

03 / REPORT

Evidence, not opinions

Findings are documented with severity, evidence, and business impact — readable by both engineers and the board.

04 / REMEDIATE

Close the gap

We support fixes through to verification, and retest where it matters, so findings actually get closed.
